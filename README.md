# Background to the Project

### 1 Project background
Udemy: SaaS learning platform empowers individuals and organizations worldwide to acquire and develop skills through flexible content created by thousands of instructors, reaching 69 million learners globally. (Udemy, 2024). Due to the growth of the learning platform and the aggressive competition between online learning platforms, Udemy has a vast number of people engaging each year but still reserves the interest to evaluate and accelerate the course quality to keep back the users and maximize their profit based on the current courses.

Udemy expects to continue expanding our consumer and enterprise customer base, instructor network, and content catalogue while increasing our market opportunities and generating long-term sustainable growth through the following strategies:

- Increase Udemy Business penetration through a land-and-expand strategy, focusing on acquiring new customers and efficiently growing relationships with current customers.

- Expand learning experiences to new modalities and more active learning that reaches a broader audience: Currently, Udemy offers powerful learning experiences for learners to prepare for certification exams and better retain learnings, which they expand to have more immersive skill assessments, lab, or cohort-based learning. 

- Optimize business model and pricing: Maximize profit by determining the optimal price we charge for our courses in our marketplace per-country basis, considering dozens of course characteristics, including content category, hours of content, course rating, and popularity.

- To support strategic decision-making, this project developed a comprehensive dashboard to analyze course performance, pricing policies, and student engagement, providing the Udemy Finance and Accounting faculty with clear, actionable insights.

### 2 Objectives and Aim
The project aims to develop a comprehensive and easily digestible finance and accounting course dashboard that analyzes Udemy's finance and accounting course situations to accelerate the system and growth strategies (Udemy, 2024). Due to the Udemy business need for the fragmented market for learning platform situation, this dashboard is designed to solve the central curiosity of "Udemy's Finance and accounting department optimizes course structure, pricing, and category to attract more students while maximizing revenue."

Objectives: 

- Visualize the Performance of Finance and Accounting Courses: Present a detailed overview of course popularity and performance by analyzing course metrics such as subscribers, ratings, and reviews, and identify top-performing courses as benchmarks of subscribers and ratings.

- Analyze the Impact of Pricing and Discounts on Revenue: Track and analyze the relationship between pricing and student enrollments to identify pricing strategies that maximize earnings, which is a drill-down view of discounted prices vs. earnings will assist the marketing team in optimizing discount levels to attract students without compromising revenue.

- Evaluate Student Engagement and Course Design: Assess the relationship between course structure (e.g., number of lectures, practice tests) and engagement metrics like reviews and subscriber counts, and help the Finance and Accounting course Department determine the optimal course design that balances content depth with user satisfaction.

- Support Data-Driven Decision-Making with Clear Insights: Ensure the dashboard is intuitively designed and accessible for both the Finance and Accounting course Department and the Marketing Team, enabling quick insights and actionable decisions, incorporating principles from Tufte (clarity and simplicity), Few (visual effectiveness), and Munzner (design validation) to ensure the dashboard communicates effectively without requiring prior data analysis training.

### 3. Audience
The primary audience for this dashboard is the Finance and Accounting course Department of Udemy, which handles course performance, publishes courses and ensures the learner's immersive online experience. 

Besides, the sub-audience of this dashboard is the marketing team, who handle marketing strategies, bursting the short and long-term growth based on the product- finance and accounting course, students' preference about the type of course, price range, etc. 

This dashboard is specially designed for executives responsible for making critical decisions about the course operation for the Finance and Accounting Course at Udemy, publishing pricing policy, and growth strategy. The dashboard will provide them with the necessary data and insights to understand the operational insights of the course offering by the Finance and Accounting department, evaluate the course quality in terms of learner rating and engagement, and identify the appropriate areas for development as well as the suitable price range to maximizing the profit. 

In short, by meeting the listed objectives, the dashboard will embrace the Udemy Finance and Accounting executives, accompanied by the marketing team, to make strategic decisions based on a data-driven method, guiding Udemy's sustainable growth for the department in the context of a desire to the development of the Udemy as a SaaS learning platform.##
